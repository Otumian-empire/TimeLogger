TimeLoggerApp

TimeLoggerApp Tracks tasks, time and developers. Every task is created by some one developer, a developer starts working on a particular or multiple tasks. Time developer invests into a task at hand is recored, how often they develop is recorded, for productivity and accountability sake. TimeLoggerApp is the so ultimate app. - Otumian
